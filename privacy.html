Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2026

Welcome to Spin A Wheel. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information processed through our web application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage on our website.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

Spin A Wheel is designed as a client-side utility application. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide any personal data (such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers) to use our interactive spin wheels.

Any custom entries, list of names, or values you input into the spinner app are processed locally within your web browser memory. This data is never uploaded, transferred, or stored on our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Our classroom spinner capabilities and general random selection features are safe for scholastic activities. Because we do not extract or archive structural personal information, we do not intentionally profile or harvest identifier information from children under the age of 13.
